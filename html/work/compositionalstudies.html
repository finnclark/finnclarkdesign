Compositional Studies
As a designer, it is important to proficiently understand the basic concept of composition. During my first year of study we were given a multitude of assignments based around this concept.

The first three images display what is known as the "strips project" where we are given a black rectangle and we have to use 90 and 45 degree angles to create a sense of positive and negative space. Following on from this is the introduction of letterforms where we explore a similar approach. The final four images displays a project where we expand on our skills by integrating duotone photographs in conjunction with letterforms.
